The Gerping-Method

Gerping is a rediscovered learning method that has been in use during the Bronze Age by the ancient Egyptians! It stands for "German Practicing" and aspires to fundamentally change the learning landscape that we know today! With these newly discovered methods, it has become unbelievably easy to brute force knowledge into our brains using "repetition". It's as simple, as it is effective!
